Welcome to Nyack, New York

Places to Go

Here is some information about my personal favorite attractions in Nyack!

Nyack Library

The Nyack Library is located on Broadway Street and contains a massive collections of all kinds of books, magazines, movies, and video games. The library provides easy access to information on the comuunity and you can get a library card for free! If your a child or teen the library occasionally has fun extracirrcular activities after school.

Memorial Park

Memorial Park is located right off of Piermont Avenue down towards the Hudson River. The park has a newly renovated playground, skate park, basketball courts, and a gazebo. The park has a pier that you can also walk out on to take pictures and enjoy the view. If you're looking for a place to have a picnic or a fun outdoor hangout spot the park is the perfect place!

Hook Mountain

The Hook Mountain State Park is located along the Palisades Ridge. The Nyack Beach State Park part of Hook Mountain is located in Upper Nyack. The are many hiking trails along Hook Mountain with amazing scenery.

Rockland Lake

Rockland Lake is located in Rockland County near Congers and Valley Cottage. The park has a pool, picnic areas, boating rentals, hiking trails, and a great path that runs along the whole lake. Many events are held here and one in particular that is coming up is the Annual Turkey Trot Race!

Palisades Mall

The Palisades Mall is located in West Nyack. It is the second-largest shopping mall in New York. The mall has over 200 stores for shopping, eating, watching movies, bowling, and ice skating.

Main Street is home to many local stores and restaurants that are special to Nyack. Some of my favorite locations is Turiellos Pizzeria, Maria Luis Boutique, and Strawberry Place. Main Street in the town of Nyack and holds many seasonal street fairs that attract lots of visitors and locals.
