Other Attractions Around Nyack, New York

Nyack is close to many other locations within driving distance that are fun places to check out!

Hudson River & Governor Mario M. Cuomo Bridge

The Hudson River flows from north to south through eastern New York. Nyack is located on the west side of the river. The Governor Mario M. Cuomo Bridge crosses over the Hudson River between Nyack and Tarrytown. There is a car, bus, walk, and bike lane for commuters to use.

New York City

New York City is about a fifty minute drive from the town of Nyack. There is lots of transportation to get there such as car, bus, or train. There are bus stops located in the town of Nyack thst can take you straight to the city. If you are looking to take the train, you can cross the Governor Mario M. Cuomo bridge and catch the train in Tarrytown! The city is a great destination if you're looking for a day of shopping, siteseeing, or just looking to meet new people for the day!
